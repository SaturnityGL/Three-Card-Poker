Instructions

Three Card Poker is played with a single 52-card deck. Place your Ante bet using the chips, then click Deal.


After seeing your cards, choose to Play (matching your Ante) or Fold and forfeit your Ante.


The dealer needs Queen-high or better to qualify. If the dealer doesn't qualify, Play bets push.
